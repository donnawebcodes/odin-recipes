Description

This creamy mushroom risotto is a savory and indulgent dish that's perfect for a cozy night in. The Arborio rice is slow-cooked to perfection in a rich blend of chicken broth, white wine, garlic, and onion, and topped with sautéed mushrooms, grated parmesan cheese, and chopped parsley. Every bite is bursting with earthy umami flavor that's sure to satisfy your taste buds.

Ingredients

  • 6 cups chicken or vegetable broth
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1/2 cup chopped onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 1/2 cups Arborio rice
  • 1/2 cup dry white wine
  • 1 cup sliced mushrooms
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Chopped fresh parsley for garnish

Steps

  1. In a large saucepan, bring the broth to a simmer over medium heat.
  2. In a separate large saucepan, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the onion and garlic and sauté until the onion is soft and translucent, about 5 minutes.
  3. Add the Arborio rice to the onion and garlic mixture and stir to coat the rice with the oil.
  4. Add the white wine and cook, stirring constantly, until the wine is absorbed by the rice.
  5. Add a ladleful of the hot broth to the rice and stir constantly until the broth is absorbed. Repeat this process, one ladleful at a time, stirring constantly and letting each ladleful of broth be absorbed before adding the next one. This process should take about 20-25 minutes.
  6. While you are adding the broth to the rice, sauté the mushrooms in a separate pan until they are browned and cooked through. Set aside.
  7. Once all the broth has been absorbed and the rice is tender, add the sautéed mushrooms, grated Parmesan cheese, salt, and pepper to the risotto. Stir until everything is combined and heated through.
  8. Serve the risotto immediately, garnished with chopped fresh parsley.

Notes

Enjoy your delicious mushroom risotto!
