Chicken Quesadilla

Description

Looking for a quick and easy meal that's packed with flavor? Look no further than these delicious chicken quesadillas! Perfect for a busy weeknight or a casual dinner party, these quesadillas are filled with tender, juicy chicken, saut√©ed peppers and onions, and gooey melted cheese, all wrapped up in a crispy, golden tortilla.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1/2 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts, diced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 green bell pepper, diced
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 large flour tortillas
  • 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • Sour cream, guacamole, and salsa (for serving)

Steps

  1. Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add the onion and garlic and cook for 2-3 minutes, until the onion is soft.
  2. Add the chicken and cook for 5-7 minutes, until the chicken is no longer pink.
  3. Add the bell peppers, chili powder, cumin, salt, and pepper, and cook for 5-7 minutes, until the peppers are tender.
  4. Place one tortilla in a large skillet or on a griddle over medium-high heat. Sprinkle 1/2 cup of cheese evenly over the tortilla, then spoon 1/4 of the chicken and pepper mixture over the cheese. Sprinkle 1 tablespoon of cilantro on top, then top with another 1/2 cup of cheese. Place another tortilla on top.
  5. Cook for 2-3 minutes, until the cheese is melted and the bottom tortilla is golden brown. Flip the quesadilla and cook for an additional 2-3 minutes, until the other side is golden brown and the cheese is melted.
  6. Repeat with the remaining tortillas and chicken mixture.
  7. Cut the quesadillas into wedges and serve with sour cream, guacamole, and salsa.

Notes

Enjoy your delicious chicken quesadillas!
